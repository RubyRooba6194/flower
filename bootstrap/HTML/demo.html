ABOUT JASC

Welcome to JASC Arts and Science College JASC Arts and science college a constituent college of JASC Group of institutions was started in the year 2005. Realizing the urgent need for a Higher Education institution in this geographical area and keeping in mind the social responsibility of building up youth our founders started this institution . JASC located in a picturesque atmosphere and tranquil surroundings has state-of-the-art infrastructure and well qualified faculty. The college offers 10 Under graduate programmes and 1 postgraduate programme. The college has an enrolment of 1548 students and 71 faculty. The institution has produced over 8000 graduates till date who are doing extremely well in various walks of their life.
